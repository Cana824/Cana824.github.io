Tā Mason Durie's Te Whare Tapa Whā model

Wall

Taha Tinana (physical wellbeing) Going to the gym 3 times a week
Sleeping an adequate amount of hours
Taha Whanau (Family Wellbeing) Make sure I catch up with family members at least once a week
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Ensuring that I share my emotions and where I feel like I am at with my wellbeing
Using breathing techniques in times of stressors
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Telling my partner and friends how much I am grateful to have them
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Taking time per week to take a good walk and connect back to nature
